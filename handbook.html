Welcome to Dolphin School

Dear Students and Parents,

Welcome to Dolphin School! We are delighted to have you as part of our educational community. This handbook serves as your comprehensive guide to understanding our school's policies, procedures, and expectations.

Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a safe, nurturing, and academically challenging environment where every student can reach their full potential.

What You'll Find in This Handbook

  • School mission, vision, and core values
  • Academic policies and grading systems
  • Attendance and punctuality requirements
  • Student code of conduct and disciplinary procedures
  • Uniform and dress code guidelines
  • School facilities and resources
  • Safety and security protocols
  • Important contact information

Please take time to read through this handbook carefully with your parents or guardians. Understanding these guidelines will help ensure a successful and enjoyable school experience.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide quality education that develops well-rounded individuals equipped with knowledge, skills, and values necessary for success in the 21st century.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution that nurtures innovative, responsible, and globally competitive citizens.

Core Values

Respect

Treating everyone with dignity and consideration

Integrity

Acting with honesty and moral principles

Excellence

Striving for the highest standards in all endeavors

Academic Policies

Grading System

Grade Scale
  • 95-100: Excellent (A)
  • 90-94: Very Good (B+)
  • 85-89: Good (B)
  • 80-84: Satisfactory (C+)
  • 75-79: Fair (C)
  • Below 75: Needs Improvement (F)

Assessment Components

30%

Written Works
Quizzes, tests, assignments

50%

Performance Tasks
Projects, presentations, lab work

20%

Quarterly Assessment
Major examinations

Homework Policy

Homework Guidelines
  • Homework should be completed on time and submitted as scheduled
  • Late submissions may result in grade deductions
  • Students are responsible for making up missed assignments
  • Parents should monitor and support homework completion

Attendance Policy

Attendance Requirement

Students must maintain at least 95% attendance rate to be eligible for promotion to the next grade level.

Absence Procedures

Excused Absences
  • Illness (with medical certificate if more than 3 days)
  • Family emergencies
  • Religious observances
  • Pre-approved educational trips

Tardiness Policy

Consequences of Tardiness
  • 3 tardies: Verbal warning
  • 5 tardies: Parent conference
  • 7 tardies: Disciplinary action
Perfect Attendance Rewards
  • Monthly recognition certificate
  • Special privileges and activities
  • Year-end perfect attendance award

Code of Conduct

Expected Behavior

Positive Behaviors
  • Show respect to teachers, staff, and fellow students
  • Follow classroom and school rules
  • Complete assignments and participate actively in class
  • Use appropriate language and behavior
  • Take care of school property and facilities

Prohibited Behaviors

Unacceptable Behaviors
  • Bullying, harassment, or discrimination
  • Violence or threats of violence
  • Cheating or academic dishonesty
  • Vandalism or destruction of property
  • Use of inappropriate language or gestures
  • Bringing prohibited items to school

Disciplinary Procedures

Progressive Discipline
  1. Verbal Warning: First offense discussion
  2. Written Warning: Documentation of behavior
  3. Parent Conference: Meeting with parents/guardians
  4. Detention: After-school or lunch detention
  5. Suspension: Temporary removal from school
  6. Expulsion: Permanent removal (severe cases)

Grooming Standards

General Guidelines
  • Hair should be neat and well-groomed
  • No extreme hairstyles or colors

School Facilities

Open Monday-Friday, 7:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Science Lab

Fully equipped for hands-on experiments

Computer Lab

30 computers with internet access

Facility Usage Rules

General Guidelines
  • Students must be accompanied by a teacher or staff member
  • Proper care and handling of equipment is required
  • Report any damage or malfunction immediately
  • Clean up after use and return materials to proper places
  • Follow specific rules for each facility

Safety & Security

Emergency Procedures

Fire Drill Procedures
  1. Stop all activities immediately when alarm sounds
  2. Follow teacher's instructions calmly
  3. Walk quickly and quietly to designated exit
  4. Assemble at assigned meeting point
  5. Wait for further instructions from teachers

Visitor Policy

Visitor Requirements
  • All visitors must register at the main office
  • Valid ID required for entry
  • Visitor's pass must be worn at all times
  • Visitors must be accompanied by school personnel
  • Sign out before leaving the premises

Health & Wellness

Health Services
  • School nurse available during school hours
  • First aid and basic medical care provided
  • Emergency contact information must be updated
  • Students with medical conditions must inform the school
  • Medication administration requires proper documentation
